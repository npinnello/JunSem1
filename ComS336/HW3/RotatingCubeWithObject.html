Keyboard controls:

  • w, a, s, d - move forward, left, back, right
  • r, f - move up, down
  • i, j, k, l - look up, turn left, look down, turn right
  • O - face origin
  • I, J, K, L - orbit down, right, up, left
  • x, X, y, Y, z, Z - positive/negative rotations about object's own axis
